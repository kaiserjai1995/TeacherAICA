Base Raised to Zero

Any real number , except 0, when raised to exponent equals to 1. (00 is an indeterminate form.)

Examples: 

	a.   50
	b.   x0
	c.  (5x)0
	d.   5x0

Solution: 

	a.   50 = 1
	b.   x0 = 1
	c.  (5x)0 = 1
	d.   5x0 = 5 • (x0) = 5 • 1 = 5

Note: Based on division, it is important to emphasize that division by zero is not defined. So x0 = 1 as long as x ≠ 0.
